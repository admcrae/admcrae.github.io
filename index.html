I am a postdoc in the OPTIM lab at the EPFL Institute of Mathematics, advised by Nicolas Boumal. Prior to that, I graduated from Georgia Tech in May 2022 with a Ph.D. in Electrical and Computer Engineering, advised by Mark Davenport.

I am generally interested in the theory of high-dimensional statistics and signal processing and machine learning, particularly from an optimization perspective. For a better idea of my current work, see my publications page or Google Scholar profile.
